# Remediation Steps

Tiered remediation recommendations with rollback plans.

## When to Use

- After root cause analysis identifies the problem
- When you need structured remediation options with risk levels
- Before executing any corrective action

## Tiers

### Tier 1: Verify
Low-risk diagnostic actions to confirm the root cause. Safe to run without approval.

### Tier 2: Mitigate
Medium-risk actions to reduce impact. May require team lead approval.

### Tier 3: High-Risk
Actions that could cause additional impact if wrong. Requires explicit approval and has rollback plan.
